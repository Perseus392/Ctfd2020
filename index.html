Capture The Flag 2020

The Event

Capture the flag is an outdoor game where two teams each having a flag have to capture the other team's flag to win the game.
An online CTF has a similar modus operandi. Complete challenges to receive hidden flags, submit said flags and gain points.
The team with the most points wins.
The challenges range from easy to moderate so even at the worst, you have something to learn.
